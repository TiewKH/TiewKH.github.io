Personality

My hobbies are reading about and exploring new technologies, playing video games and watching anime. I am a meticulous person and prefer to get things done as close to perfect as possible on the first try. I like to prioritise tasks and strive to get things necessary to be done, completed first. I feel the best when I know I'm improving. I am mildly competitive and do not like to lose but even if I do, my main priority will always be self-improvement. What better way is there to improve than to fail? However, failing is good only if you fail early and the damage done is negligible. I am not fond of doing things during the eleventh hour anyway, so this blends well with my workflow.
